Hi, I am Vihang Patil. I am a Ph.D candidate in Machine Learning at [Institute for Machine Learning](https://www.jku.at/en/institute-for-machine-learning/), JKU Linz, Austria and I am advised by Prof. Sepp Hochreiter.

My life goal is to push boundaries of humanity and my current area of interest to do this is through research in learning systems/machine learning/reinforcement learning. My research revolves around long term credit assignment in Reinforcement Learning and how we can build algorithms which learn to build temporal abstractions to learn faster and generalise to unknown environments.

I completed my Bachelors in Engineering from University of Mumbai, where I built various kinds of robots (some of which played [badminton](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wfj4C9W8Lp8)!). This experience of building robots for nearly three years was quite important to me. We build robots which were manually controlled and had to be reprogrammed for new problems. This lack of transfer to new problems was the reason I moved towards developing learning algorithms, in hope that we have someday physical systems which are more adaptable to new problems.  

Further, I completed my master\'s degree in Artificial Intelligence from USI, Switzerland. During which I spent a semester studying and further also completed my master's thesis under the supervision of [Dr. Aurelien Lucchi](http://people.inf.ethz.ch/alucchi/) at ETH, Zurich.
